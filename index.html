About Me

I’m a third year Astrophysics and Computer Science student at George Washington University who enjoys working with mixed reality. In my free time, I explore the intersection between astronomy and computer graphics with specific interest in recreating high energy phenomena of the cosmos. I’ve given talks and presentations on this work. I started and currently work on the Teaching Astrophysics in Virtual Reality project at GWU and have experience with software consulting.
